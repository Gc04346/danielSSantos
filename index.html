June 2018 - Current

15° FLISoL Organization

The latin american free software installation festival (FLISoL) happens yearly througout all Latin America meaning to motivate the usage of free software, as well as disseminate knowledge about those. The 15ª edition of this event, at Belo Horizonte, was hosted by CEFET-MG. I had the privilege of being the organizer of this festival, leading a team with 13 people(COMPET members). We prepared an event with over 30 workshops, kids playground, robotics workshops, an InstallFest, a programming marathon, and a closing with the participation of the college band! All that for a target audience of 500 people! The preparation envolved the creation and maintaining of the event at an online tool called Sympla, disclosure material creation, sponsorship searching, making thematic t-shirts and stickers, panelist searching, reservation of rooms and spaces at the campus, and sooo much more! All that wouldn't be possible without the commitment and cooperation of the whole team! We even got a post at CEFET-MG news blog!
